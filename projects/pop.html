POP turns hand-drawn wireframes to interactive prototypes. Sketch the app ideas on paper; take pictures and add links between the mockups; sync your app prototypes with Dropbox, simulate on iPhone, Android, or web and share your prototype idea online to get feedbacks.

I led design and collaborated closely with the developer team to create a prototyping tool that works on web & mobile. Developing the idea into a design which included user research, wireframes, user flows, concept designs, mockups, prototypes, visual design and branding.
